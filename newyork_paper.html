The first cannabis store to open in New York

Edan Nguyễn

The first licensed cannabis store in New York , USA opened on December 29, more than a year after the state officially legalized marijuana.
Housing Works cannabis store employees arrange products before opening on December 29
According to Reuters, New York 's first licensed cannabis store opened on December 29 at the East Village residential area in New York City. The store is owned by Housing Works, a nonprofit that helps reduce homelessness and HIV/ AIDS.

Housing Works was among the first 36 organizations and individuals to receive a retail marijuana license from the state of New York last month.

"We are prioritizing redressing the harm, even the harm, caused by state policy . It is no coincidence that those incarcerated for possession and sale of drugs are largely black and Latino," said Chris Alexander, newly inaugurated CEO of the New York State Office of Cannabis Administration. , said during a press conference at the store.
Customers wait in line to enter the first legal marijuana store in New York on December 29
The store opened more than a year after the state of New York officially legalized marijuana in March 2021. New York congressmen stipulate that marijuana can only be sold by licensed retailers and to adults over the age of 21. The first licenses will be given to entrepreneurs who have been arrested or convicted in connection with cannabis to give them an edge over retailers in this lucrative market. Retailers can also only sell cannabis grown and processed by licensed New York producers.

Initially, New York State promised to find store space and business loans for the first licensees. Non-profit groups that work with former incarcerated people, such as Housing Works, may be eligible for some permits. However, the process took longer than expected.

Marijuana has also been fully legalized by 20 other states in the US. However, under federal law, marijuana is still illegal. This can make it difficult for providers to access business loans and other banking services.

At Housing Works, several types of cannabis are sold for $20-30 for about 3.5g. The rolls available here are made from cannabis produced by Florist Farms in upstate New York.
Customers wait in line to enter the first legal marijuana store in New York on December 29
“This is a big change for our company. We will be able to hire more people,” said Karli Miller-Hornick, co-founder of Florist Farms.

Cannabis sales in New York will be taxed at 13.5%. This tax will be used for schools, public housing, addiction services and mental health services.
