Sparkling festival 'Noel Sydney'

Thanh Tu

According to the Vietnam News Agency correspondent in Sydney, these days, Sydney's downtown area is filled with lights with all kinds of shimmering colors in a vibrant atmosphere thanks to the "Noel Sydney" festival.
Festival 'Noel Sydney. Photo: concreteplayground.com
This is a large and completely free Christmas festival, held for 16 nights (from December 9 to 24) with many exciting activities such as drone show, Christmas forests by brilliant LED lights, and many other interesting and engaging experiences.

When coming to the "Noel Sydney" festival, visitors will be overwhelmed by the shimmering and majestic beauty of hundreds of drones performing in the sky, carrying a "mission" to tell Christmas stories. traditional birth. This festive atmosphere spreads from Macquarie Street, famous for its splendid sandstone historic buildings, to the Royal Botanic Gardens, one of the largest in the city center. Sydney. The entire road connecting these two famous places has been lit up by "Noel Sydney" with beautiful light installations.

Besides, "Noel Sydney" is also attractive thanks to the attraction from the bustling Christmas market, bustling stalls, street food space and indispensable nightly art performances. Coming to the "Noel Sydney" festival, visitors are not only mesmerized by the magnificent lights but also conquered by the beauty of the towering digital Christmas forest, along with live music performances. .
The buildings are lit with LED lights. (Photo: Thanh Tu/VNA)
Designed with the hope of reinvigorating, revitalizing the bustling life in one of Australia's largest cities and attracting tourists again after 3 years of silence due to the COVID-19 pandemic, the festival " Noel Sydney" is considered as a new wind and an attractive destination in this year's Christmas season in "kangaroo land". The festival offers Australian families and visitors a safe and memorable Christmas, with hopes for a good New Year ahead.

According to New South Wales Tourism Department Director, Ben Franklin, the "Noel Sydney" festival will bring magic to the city center of Sydney, creating a lot of joy and happiness for Australians as well as visitors. He hopes there will be more events like “Noel Sydney” to attract more visitors to Sydney, thereby supporting businesses, creating more jobs and motivating businesses in the commercial district.
