Messi's is about to appear on Argentina's banknotes

Edan Nguyễn

According to the Argentine press, the Central Bank of Argentina has planned to put the image of L. Messi on the 1000 peso coin to celebrate the achievement of winning the 2022 World Cup gold cup.
Image of Messi receiving the World Cup title with Argentina
Due to its extremely important contribution to La Albiceleste on the way to the 2022 World Cup gold cup, the Argentine central bank recently had an emergency meeting to discuss many important issues such as bonuses for the team, how to pay tribute to the team and the plan to print Messi's picture on the 1,000 peso note, which is the highest denomination sheet in this country.

As expected, the front of this banknote will be printed with the image of Lionel Messi, the back will be printed with the image of coach Lionel Scaloni. This is the idea put forward by Eduardo Hecker, president of the Central Bank of Argentina, and it is likely that this particular banknote will be limitedly printed.

It is known that in history, only 2 times Argentina did this with the first time being the 1978 World Cup victory, the next time celebrating the 50th anniversary of the death of Eva Peron (the late first lady of Argentina).
Messi's picture appears on the money
Many supported the plan, particularly governor Lisandro Cleri. But there are also objections because they believe that in the context of escalating inflation, the cost of producing a new banknote will be very expensive, affecting the overall budget. But the majority of supporters believe that this is the least they can do for the national team, especially Messi.

Besides, the parties also discussed a tribute to the national team. But this is very difficult as Albiceleste members will have very little time at home. And they are certainly very busy with the celebratory events after the championship.
